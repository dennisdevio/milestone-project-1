About us

The Golden Pretzel is an inviting and cozy café in the heart of Stockholm. We served newly baked bread and pastries with a Swedish flair from our bakery with only the highest quality ingredients available.

We are a small family owned business with the passion of bringing you the best bread and pastries Stockholm has to offer.

We welcome you to visit us!
